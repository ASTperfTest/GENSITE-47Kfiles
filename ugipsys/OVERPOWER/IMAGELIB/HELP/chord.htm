CHORD

Draws a closed figure represented by the intersection of a line and an ellipse.

procedure Chord(x1: Integer, y1: Integer, x2: Integer, y2:Integer, x3: Integer, y3: Integer, x4: Integer, y4:Integer)

Description
Use Chord to create a shape that is defined by an arc and a line that joins the endpoints of the arc. The chord consists of a portion of an ellipse that is bounded by the points (X1,Y1) and (X2,Y2). The ellipse is bisected by a line that runs between the points (X3,Y3) and (X4,Y4). The perimeter of the chord runs counter clockwise from (X3, Y3), counterclockwise along the ellipse to (X4,Y4), and straight back to (X3,Y3). If (X3,Y3) and (X4,Y4) are not on the surface of the ellipse, the corresponding corners on the chord are the closest points on the perimeter that intersect the line. The outline of the chord is drawn using the values of PenColor, PenStyle and PenMode, and the shape is filled using the value of BrushColor, BrushStyle.
