PENMODE

Determines how the pen draws lines on the canvas.

property PenMode: Integer

Description
Set Mode to determine how the color of the pen interacts with the color on the image. The effects of Mode are described in the following table:

Value Pixels color
1 (Black) Always black
2 (White) Always white
3 (Nop) Unchanged
4 (Not) Inverse of canvas background color
5 (Copy) Pen color specified in Color property (Default)
6 (Not Copy) Inverse of pen color
7 (Merge pen not) Combination of pen color and inverse of canvas background
8 (Mask pen Not) Combination of colors common to both pen and inverse of canvas background.
9 (Merge not pen) Combination of canvas background color and inverse of pen color
10 (Mask not Pen) Combination of colors common to both canvas background and inverse of pen
11 (Merge) Combination of pen color and canvas background color
12 (Not merge) Inverse of pmMerge: combination of pen color and canvas background color
13 (Mask) Combination of colors common to both pen and canvas background
14 (Not mask) Inverse of pmMask: combination of colors common to both pen and canvas background
15 (Xor) Combination of colors in either pen or canvas background, but not both
16 (Not xor) Inverse of pmXor: combination of colors in either pen or canvas background, but not both

Note:    The default value is 5 (Copy)
