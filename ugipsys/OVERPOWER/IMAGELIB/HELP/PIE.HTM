PIE

Draws a pie-shaped the section of the ellipse bounded by the rectangle (X1, Y1) and (X2, Y2).

procedure Chord(x1: Integer, y1: Integer, x2: Integer, y2:Integer, x3: Integer, y3: Integer, x4: Integer, y4:Integer)

Description
Use Pie to draw a pie-shaped wedge on the image. The wedge is defined by the ellipse bounded by the rectangle determined by the points (X1, Y1) and X2, Y2). The section drawn is determined by two lines radiating from the center of the ellipse through the points (X3, Y3) and (X4, Y4).

The outline of the ellipse is drawn using the values of PenColor, PenStyle and PenMode, and the shape is filled using the value of BrushColor, BrushStyle.
