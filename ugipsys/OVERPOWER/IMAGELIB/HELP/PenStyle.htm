PENSTYLE

Determine the style in which the pen draws lines or borders.

property PenStyle: Integer

Description
Use Style to draw a dotted or dashed line, or to omit the line that appears as a frame around shapes. These are the possible values of Style:

Value Meaning
1 (Solid) A solid line
2 (Dash) A line made up of a series of dashes
3 (Dot) A line made up a series of dots
4 (Dashdot) A line made up of alterning dashes and dots
5 (DashDotDot) A line made up of a series of dash-dot-dot combinations
6 (Clear) No line is draw (Used to omit the line around shapes that draw an outline using the current pen)
7 (Insideframe) A solid line, but one that may use a dithered color if PenWidth is greater than 1.

Note:    Styles 2 to 5 only avaliable when Penwidth is 1
